About Olympian Health Solutions

Olympian Health Solutions was founded by a Doctor of Physical Therapy student, certified personal trainer, and nutrition coach with a passion for bringing clinical-grade exercise interventions to individuals at home or online.

With a background in exercise physiology and pain science research, our approach integrates the latest scientific evidence to create programs that address not only fitness goals, but also chronic conditions, post-injury rehabilitation, and functional independence for at-risk populations.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class, prescriptive exercise and nutritional guidance to individuals seeking a higher quality of life. We believe that movement is medicine, and our mission is to make expert-guided movement accessible to everyone—no matter their location or limitations.

Our Values

  • Evidence-Based Practice: Grounded in current research and best clinical practices.
  • Holistic Care: Addressing the whole person—body, mind, and lifestyle.
  • Collaboration: Working alongside healthcare professionals to ensure continuity of care.
  • Empowerment: Educating clients to take charge of their own health journey.
